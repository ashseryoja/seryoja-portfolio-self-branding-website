## Personal story
I grew up in Novomoskovsk and lived in that area until I was 15. I went to school №6
there. Then I moved to Armenia, enrolled in school №147, and finished it there.

After school I went to a medical college and studied to be a dental technician. While I
was at the medical college, programming was my hobby on the side. The hobby ended up
beating the day job — I dropped out of medicine and started doing what I actually loved.

After that I enrolled at the National Polytechnical University of Armenia (NPUA), faculty
of Software Engineering, where I'm still studying.

## Where I am now
I'm a Full-Stack Developer, AI Integrator, and AI Automation Engineer. I build AI
integrations and automation systems that connect products, data, teams, and business
workflows. I can take an idea from prototype to production: interfaces, APIs, agents,
payments, analytics, and deployment. Currently I'm building UpSound, an AI platform for
